    Integrating Slack

    Overview

    This section will teach you how to connect Trello to your Slack account in order to send cards with tasks to a channel or person in Slack. Before starting, ensure you are at the Trello home page.

    Add Slack to Trello

    1. Open your board in Trello.

    2. Click the icon in the top-right menu, then click [Add Power-Ups] within the pop-up.

    3. Search for Slack using the search bar.

      Info

      Trello has many apps and websites you can integrate and connect to. Visit this trello integrations page to learn more.

    4. Once found, click on Slack.

    5. Click [Add].

    6. Sign in to your Slack workspace, if no such prompt appears, you may skip this step and skip step 6.

    7. Authorize Trello to access your Slack account.

    Success

    You should now see the word "Slack" and its logo in the top right menu.

    Send Cards to Slack

    1. Click any Trello card.

    2. Click [Power-ups] on the card.

      Warning

      If your pop-up looks like the image below after completing step 2, then you must click [Link Your Trello Account] and follow those steps before proceeding. See the troubleshooting page for more info.

    3. Click Slack.

    4. Choose a channel or user.

      Info

      If you are unfamiliar with Slack and do not know where to send your card, consider visiting this Slack information page

    5. Click [Send] to share the card in Slack.

    Success

    You should now see a message detailing the Trello card in whatever slack channel or user you sent it to.

    Conclusion

    If you have followed these instructions, you now have your Trello account linked to Slack and can readily send cards to users or channels.

    Success

    You can now send cards of tasks to Slack, allowing for better communication and clarity between you and team members.
