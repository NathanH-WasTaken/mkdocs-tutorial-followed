# Troubleshooting

| Symptoms      | Probable Cause | Action    |
|------|------------|-----|
| Member not appearing in shared Trello board. | Board invite has not been accepted. | **Click** [Share], enter their email, and then send the invite. |
| Unable to invite people to Trello board. | You do not have permission to invite others. | Ask the Trello board owner to grant permissions. |
| Unable to add Slack in Power-Ups. | You do not have permission to add Power-Ups to the board. | Ask the Trello board owner to add it themselves or grant permissions. |
| [Link Your Trello Account] prompt appears. | Your Trello and Slack accounts have not been linked. | **Click** [Link Your Trello Account] and follow the steps. |
